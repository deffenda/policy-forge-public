# Auditability

Policy Forge is designed around immutable evidence and append-only audit history.

## Evidence

Evidence is captured as a source record with metadata describing what it supports, when it was collected, who submitted it, and which findings, controls, or package artifacts it relates to.

## Audit History

Material workflow events are appended to history instead of overwritten. Reviewers can see when status changed, why it changed, and which source records supported the decision.

## Dashboards

Executive views are not standalone summaries. They drill down to source records so leadership can inspect the basis for posture, trends, regressions, and authorization readiness.
