# ISSO Workflow

ISSOs use Policy Forge to validate remediation, maintain POA&M integrity, and attest to control impact.

1. Review findings marked ready for validation.
2. Inspect remediation evidence and source scan context.
3. Confirm whether the POA&M item can be updated.
4. Record attestation with rationale.
5. Update control posture through a traceable workflow.
6. Prepare assessment-ready records.

The ISSO workflow preserves compliance accountability without blocking engineering remediation.
