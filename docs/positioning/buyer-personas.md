# Buyer Personas

## CISO

Needs continuous visibility, defensible posture reporting, and evidence-backed executive views.

## Authorizing Official

Needs authorization packages that can be traced to current records and risk decisions.

## ISSO

Needs efficient attestation workflows, POA&M traceability, and control history.

## Assessor

Needs reviewable evidence, clear control mappings, and immutable audit history.

## Cloud Engineer

Needs to remediate quickly without waiting for compliance gates.
