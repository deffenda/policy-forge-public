# CISO Brief

## Executive View

Policy Forge is security-led, evidence-backed compliance automation for regulated cloud environments. It is designed for organizations that need defensible authorization packages, continuous monitoring, and audit-ready evidence without slowing the engineers responsible for reducing risk.

Most compliance programs fail operationally when evidence, POA&Ms, control status, and assessments live in disconnected tools. Policy Forge aligns those records around the real security work: scanner findings, remediation actions, validation evidence, risk decisions, and authorization artifacts.

## What Changes

Engineers remediate risk first. ISSOs and compliance staff validate the work asynchronously. Assessors review source evidence instead of reconstructed summaries. AOs receive packages assembled from auditable records. CISOs see posture that can be drilled down to the finding, evidence, POA&M, control, and assessment level.

## Why It Matters

- Faster remediation of material risk.
- Less audit scramble before assessments.
- Better traceability from technical findings to executive risk decisions.
- Stronger confidence in control posture.
- Reduced dependence on slideware, spreadsheets, and manually curated dashboards.

## Operating Model

Policy Forge supports a shared workflow:

1. Scanner tools produce findings.
2. Findings are deduplicated and mapped to controls.
3. Engineers remediate or document risk treatment.
4. Evidence is captured as immutable source material.
5. ISSOs validate remediation and attest to control impact.
6. Assessors review evidence and control history.
7. Authorization packages are assembled from the underlying records.

## Buyer Outcomes

CISOs gain visibility into risk and control posture. AOs receive defensible authorization materials. ISSOs reduce manual reconciliation. Assessors get traceable evidence. Cloud engineers fix issues without waiting for compliance gates.
