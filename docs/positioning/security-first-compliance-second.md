# Security First, Compliance With Proof

Policy Forge is built on a simple principle: compliance should be backed by proof.

In regulated environments, teams sometimes wait for compliance review before taking action. That delay can preserve process while extending exposure. Policy Forge keeps remediation moving while preserving strong compliance accountability.

## Practical Meaning

- Engineers can fix critical findings immediately.
- Compliance review happens against captured source records.
- Evidence is preserved when the work occurs.
- POA&Ms remain tied to real findings.
- Control status changes require traceable rationale.

## Human Accountability

Automation can reduce manual effort, but accountability remains human. AI-assisted workflows can draft summaries, suggest mappings, and prepare review material. They do not silently change compliance status or become the authoritative record.
